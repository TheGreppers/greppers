  <div class="sfi-page-header-content">
    <h1>Services & <span class="gold">Programs</span></h1>
    <p>
      From standards development to hands-on testing and professional certification,
      SFI offers comprehensive services for the motorsports industry.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>

<section class="sfi-sect">
  <div class="sfi-sect-label">What We Offer</div>
  <div class="sfi-sect-title">Comprehensive Safety Services</div>
  <div class="sfi-sect-desc">
    SFI's programs are open to all interested parties, use technical committees with
    cross-sector expertise, and follow due process procedures.
  </div>

  <div class="sfi-info-grid">
    <div class="sfi-info-card">
      <h3>Standards Development</h3>
      <p>
        SFI specifications are developed through an open, transparent process involving
        manufacturers, sanctioning bodies, and technical experts. All specifications
        are publicly available.
      </p>
      <p>
        Equipment manufacturers test and self-certify their products to SFI standards,
        which are then adopted by race sanctioning organizations into their rulebooks.
      </p>
    </div>
    <div class="sfi-info-card">
      <h3>Test Laboratory</h3>
      <p>
        The SFI Test Lab in Poway, CA offers Thermal Protective Performance (TPP) testing,
        flammability tests, seatbelt assembly strength testing, mechanical properties testing,
        and custom fixture development.
      </p>
      <p>
        Our lab simulates real-world motorsport conditions to ensure equipment meets
        the highest safety standards before reaching racers.
      </p>
    </div>
    <div class="sfi-info-card">
      <h3>Quality Monitoring</h3>
      <p>
        SFI monitors adherence to specifications through certified test reports, periodic
        reviews, and contractual licensing agreements with inspection provisions.
      </p>
      <p>
        We actively investigate and publish notices about counterfeit labels and
        non-compliant products to protect the motorsports community.
      </p>
    </div>
    <div class="sfi-info-card">
      <h3>Certification & Training</h3>
      <p>
        Our Tech Inspector Certification program trains professionals to evaluate
        safety equipment compliance at racing events across the country.
      </p>
      <p>
        The Incident Response Training program prepares safety personnel for emergency
        situations at motorsports events.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="sfi-sect">
  <div class="sfi-sect-label">Testing</div>
  <div class="sfi-sect-title">Test Lab Capabilities</div>
  <div class="sfi-sect-desc">
    Opened in 1999 and now located in Poway, CA, the SFI Test Laboratory serves
    manufacturers' testing needs with professional-grade equipment.
  </div>

  <div class="sfi-features">
    <div class="sfi-feature-card">
      <div class="sfi-feature-icon">&#128293;</div>
      <h3>Thermal Testing</h3>
      <p>Thermal Protective Performance (TPP) testing and flammability tests for driver suits, gloves, hoods, and other protective gear.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="sfi-feature-card">
      <div class="sfi-feature-icon">&#128170;</div>
      <h3>Strength Testing</h3>
      <p>Seatbelt assembly strength testing and mechanical properties testing to ensure restraint systems meet safety requirements.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="sfi-feature-card">
      <div class="sfi-feature-icon">&#128736;&#65039;</div>
      <h3>Custom Fixtures</h3>
      <p>Custom fixture development for specialized equipment testing, including stock car type racing seats and unique component geometries.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
