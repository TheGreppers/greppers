How It Fits Together

Architecture at a Glance

Every user flow in the prototype — driver, inspector, or staff — routes through one login layer, one data layer, and one admin console. The AI and gamification surfaces ride on top of the same Flask API, so spec changes propagate instantly across every feature.

Below is the full prototype at a glance: users on the left, the surfaces they touch in the middle, and the non-profit's control loop on the right.
